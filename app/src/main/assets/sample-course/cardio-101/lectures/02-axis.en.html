Cardiac Axis

The mean QRS axis normally lies between −30° and +90°. It can be estimated from leads I and aVF:

Inline: $\theta = \arctan(aVF / I)$.

$$ \theta = \arctan\!\left(\frac{aVF}{I}\right) $$
